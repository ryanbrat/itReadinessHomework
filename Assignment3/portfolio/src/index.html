This is my rad side

This is my rad side. I have been a musician, a mechanic, and an auto enthusiast. But one of my greatest passions is biking. Shortly after getting into mountain biking with my father at a young age I discovered trials. This form of biking is obstacle oriented and requires precision, balance and power. I loved the challenge and began competing at a young age. This experience and over 10 years of training has earned me the current two time national champion title. But for me it doesn't end there. Over the years I have performed stunts shows at different festivals and events, and noticed young kids reaction is almost always, "WOW!". I realized I could combine a positive message with my stunt show to inspire the next generation. Over the past few years I have begun booking school presentations, this is where I want to shift my focus as a rider.
